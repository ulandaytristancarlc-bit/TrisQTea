About Us

At TrisQTea, we believe milk tea should be more than just a drink — it should be a moment of happiness. Our brand was created with a passion for crafting refreshing beverages that blend quality, flavor, and creativity in every cup.

We use carefully selected tea leaves, premium ingredients, and fresh add-ons to bring out the perfect balance of sweetness, creaminess, and aroma. Whether you’re craving classic milk tea, fruity blends, or something uniquely you, TrisQTea serves each drink with care and love.

TrisQTea isn’t just a milk tea shop — it’s your go-to chill spot, your comfort drink, and your everyday treat. Sip, enjoy, and experience the Q-Tea Quality you deserve.

Our Mission

    At TrisQTea, our mission is to serve high-quality, freshly crafted milk tea that brings comfort, joy, and unforgettable flavor to every customer. We aim to create a drink experience that blends creativity, authenticity, and passion in every cup.
